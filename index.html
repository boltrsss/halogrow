It's better than a hair transplant! How I saved my hair despite genetics and age

One simple step - great results!

Hello! My name is Emma C., I am 46 years old, and I have been a hair stylist for most of my life. Despite all my knowledge and experience, I faced serious problems with my hair. Popular products turned out to be useless, and I had to look for another way to save my hair. Today I want to tell you how I completely restored and even significantly improved the condition of my hair in a short period, without dangerous procedures and expensive drugs!

As a stylist, I have always prided myself on my hair care skills, but this did not help to avoid disaster after 40. At first, my hair began to thin gradually, but I did not pay attention to it, thinking that the problem would go away on its own. I continued to use regular hair care products, hoping that they would cope.

However, after 2-3 months, the loss became more noticeable, my hair became drier, and I started going grey earlier than my friends. I decided to try other professional products and supplements, but there was no noticeable improvement. The worst thing was that my hair loss became obvious to others. As a stylist, it was difficult for me to come to terms with the fact that I could not cope with my hair. Sometimes I just wanted to stay home, avoid meetings and communication with clients.

Of course, at first I did not accept this situation. Imagine, I am only 46 years old, I have been working with hair all my life, and I started having such problems with my hair. I understand that there are risks at this age, but I also hoped that I would never face such a problem. I take good care of my hair. I use quality cosmetics, eat nutritious food. And I did not avoid problems.

But work is work, and I had to do my duties. I started wearing a scarf on my head or making a bun so that the condition of my hair was not visible. At the same time, I was looking for ways to stop hair loss and restore the previous condition of my hair.

A trichologist will not help you...

In despair, I turned to a trichologist. I was prescribed a special medicated shampoo, which had to be used for two weeks, and vitamins. However, despite all my efforts, the hair loss continued to increase, which plunged me into a state of despair. The doctor said that if it worsened, I would need laser hair therapy. And this was what scared me the most.

Conducting laser therapy.

At home, I looked for more information about alopecia in women. It develops a little differently in us than in men and it is not necessarily bald spots, but it can simply be increased hair loss and deterioration in hair quality and this is also alopecia. If you have begun to notice that your hair has become brittle, dry, has lost its shine and is falling out in greater quantities, then these are the first signs of alopecia. It is possible to prevent alopecia and I will tell you how.

Barely noticeable alopecia: dry and dull hair, gray hair, hair loss.
